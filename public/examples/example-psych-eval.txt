PSYCHIATRIC EVALUATION (Example)
Patient: Jane Doe (pseudonym)
Date: 2024-11-15

Chief complaint:
"I can't sleep and I've been feeling hopeless."

HPI:
Patient reports depressed mood for 3 months, anhedonia, low energy, poor concentration, and insomnia. Appetite decreased with 6-lb weight loss. Passive suicidal ideation last month; denies plan or intent. Anxiety increased since job loss. Denies manic symptoms, psychosis, or panic attacks.

Mental status exam:
Appearance appropriate, mood "down", affect constricted. Thought process linear. No hallucinations or delusions. Insight and judgment fair.

PHQ-9:
Score 18 (moderately severe). Endorsed low interest, depressed mood, sleep disturbance, fatigue, poor appetite, difficulty concentrating.

Assessment:
Major Depressive Disorder, recurrent, moderate. Generalized Anxiety Disorder. Rule out PTSD.

Plan:
Increase sertraline to 100 mg daily; continue trazodone 50 mg nightly PRN. Start weekly therapy. Safety plan reviewed.
